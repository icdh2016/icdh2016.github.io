General Information

   Please read the following Paper Submission Guideline carefully to verify that your paper draft is formatted correctly and you have all the requested information before submission.

    Submissions are electronic only to this websitewww.smartchair.org/icdh2016/

Submission Requirements

Length: The manuscript must be not less than 4 pages, including all text, figures, and references.

Language: The manuscript must be written in English.

Template: We recommend that you use the Word file or LaTeX files to produce your document, since they have been set up to meet the formats. The LaTeX environment files specify suitable margins,   page layout, text, and a bibliography style. The following style files and templates are available for users of LaTeX and Microsoft Word.

  • latex-templates.zip - LaTeX style file along with an example template and  resulting PDF.
  • word-template.doc - Word 97/2000 template of correct formatting and font use.

File Format: Manuscripts must be submitted in Adobe’s Portable Document Format (PDF) format.

File Size: The manuscript must not exceed 10 MB (megabytes) in size.

Policies

Single Blind Review: We use single-blind peer review so that the authors should provide their affiliation, email address, mailing address in the submission paper. 

Dual Submissions: By submitting a manuscript to ICDH2016, the authors guarantee that it has not been previously published (or accepted for publication) in substantially similar  form. The authors also attest that they did not submit substantially similar submissions to ICDH2016. Violation of any of these conditions will lead to rejection.
