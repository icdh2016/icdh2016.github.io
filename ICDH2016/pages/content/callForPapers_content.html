General Information

   The 6th International Conference on Digital Home, with the theme of “Digital Home, Better Life”, is a premier forum for the presentation of technological advances and research results in the fields of digital application in daily life. It will bring together leading engineers and scientists in graphics and image processing, network communications, and computer technologies from around the world. It will be a platform for communication and share of the cutting-edge ideas and technologies in digital home development. The previous conferences were held successively in 2006, 2008, 2010, 2012 and 2014, and played a good role in promoting the development of the digital home industry.

   Selected best papers will be recommended to the special issues of some SCI journals and EI journals. 

Topics

   The conference, focusing on the critical theories and technologies in digital home, includes, but not limited to, the following areas.

  • Computer Graphics
  • Computer Vision & Image Processing
  • Multimedia Signal Processing
  • Digital Home Standards & Service
  • Modeling & Simulation
  • Data Mining
  • Big Data Storage, Retrieval & Visualization
  • Cloud Computing & Super Computing
  • Software Engineering & Embedded System
  • Wireless Network & Smart Home Gateway
  • User Interaction & Games
  • Virtual Reality & Virtual Materials

Paper Submission

   The manuscript must be no less than 4 pages, including all text, figures, and references, written in English. Submissions are electronic only to this website www.smartchair.org/icdh2016/.
