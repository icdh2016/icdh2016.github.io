General Instructions:

   The regular length is within 6 pages including all figures, tables, and references. The length limit is 10 pages. Each additional page beyond 6 pages will be charged US$50. Camera-ready paper submission instructions are now located at http://www.ieeeconfpublishing.org/cpir/authorKit.asp?Facility=CPS_Dec&ERoom=ICDH+2016. The journal or special issue papers need to be at least 30% different from the papers published in the ICDH 2016 conference proceedings. If your paper is selected to the journals or special issue, you need to extend it to meet the requirement.
